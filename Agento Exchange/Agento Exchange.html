AgentoExchange


This tool will automatically select ADSL Exchange in Agento tickets so all that you have to do is to Drag & Drop the AgentoExchange link above to your Bookmarks Toolbar or follow the below instructions to get it working.

Notes to take into consideration:

  • DON`T WRITE DOWN EXCHANGE NAME IT'S CASE SENSITIVE, COPY IT FROM MATRIX or ORDERS BUT KNOW THAT IT`S NOT ALWAYS RIGHT

  • Firefox 30 or greater is required,
  • Our Proxy server must be used: gawpad/proxy.pac
  • This will work on Agento while creating or updating Tickets that have ADSL Exchange Choice only
  • Exchange Not Found mail is sent as per KMP General Info section #14
